    About Us

    µLearn VJCET is the official µLearn campus community of Viswajyothi College of Engineering and Technology (VJCET),established in August 2025.Founded with the vision of creating a vibrant learning ecosystem,the chapter was introduced to the campus through the efforts of Mr.Roshan Johnson (2025 Campus Lead) and the staff advisor (Staff advisor name). We are a student-driven community dedicated to promoting peer-to-peer learning,innovation,collaboration,and skill development.Through workshops,hackathons,learning circles,mentorship programs,and community initiatives,we provide students with opportunities to explore technology,build meaningful projects,and develop industry-relevant skills. At µLearn VJCET,we believe that learning extends beyond classrooms.Our mission is to cultivate a culture where students learn together,share knowledge,support one another,and grow into future-ready professionals.By connecting learners,mentors,and industry experts,we strive to empower every student to unlock their full potential and contribute positively to the tech community.
